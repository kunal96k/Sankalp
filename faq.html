What types of properties does Sankalp Landmark offer?
Sankalp Landmark specializes in luxury residential properties, including premium apartments, villas, and penthouses. Our projects are known for their modern architecture, high-quality construction, and world-class amenities. We currently have projects like Mountain Bliss, Sunscape, and Beautiful-Bhavali in our portfolio.
What is the booking process for a Sankalp Landmark property?
The booking process is simple and transparent. You can start by visiting our website and filling out the inquiry form, or by calling us at +91 8007 751 751. Our sales team will guide you through property selection, documentation, and payment plans. We offer flexible payment options and ensure a hassle-free booking experience.
What amenities are included in Sankalp Landmark properties?
Our properties come with a comprehensive range of amenities including 24/7 security, landscaped gardens, children's play area, clubhouse, swimming pool, gymnasium, indoor games room, party hall, and dedicated parking spaces. We also ensure eco-friendly features like rainwater harvesting and solar power integration.
What are the payment plans available?
We offer multiple payment options to suit your needs:
  • Down payment with easy EMI options
  • Construction-linked payment plans
  • Flexible payment schedules
  • Bank loan assistance
Our team will work with you to find the most suitable payment plan for your requirements.

  • 5-year structural warranty
We believe in transparency and regularly update our customers about construction progress.
